    Oprah Winfrey aka Oprah Gail Winfrey, is an American tv host, actress, producer, media executive and author. As of 2023, Oprah Winfrey’s net worth is $3.5 Billion. She is most known for her chat show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, which originated in Chicago.

    She is known as the “Queen of All Media,” she was the wealthiest African-American of the twentieth century, the world’s first black billionaire, and the biggest black philanthropist in United States history. By 2007, she was often regarded as the world’s most powerful woman.
