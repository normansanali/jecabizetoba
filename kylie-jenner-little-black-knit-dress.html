    Kylie Jenner is continuing her sultry streak of little black minidresses.

    In an Instagram photo dump posted yesterday, the makeup mogul shared a collection of recent happy moments: glimpses of daily life with her kids, snapshots of family dinner, an intimate photo of her taking a relaxing bubble bath, shots of sisters Kim and Khloé Kardashian, and of course, some selfies and looks at her latest outfits.

    One picture—a mirror selfie—shows Jenner wearing a black knit minidress that featured open slits, loose strands, large cutouts, and flower-like embellishments. It also included a halter neckline that doubled as a choker. Keeping the attention on the intricate details of the dress, Jenner accessorized with small gold zigzag earrings and opted for a simple French manicure, rosy makeup, a maroon lip, and a slicked-back bun.

    "Quick update," Jenner captioned the post.

    Instagram

    The Kylie Lip Kit founder has been on a Wednesday-core style streak this year. Her shredded LBD moment comes a month after she stepped out in another unique all-black look for a dinner at The Nice Guy in Los Angeles, where she celebrated the 40th birthdays family friends Malika and Khadijah Haqq. The party outfit was composed of a sheer mesh top with black paneling at the bust from Acne Studios, a black satin mini skirt, black sheer tights, matching pointed-toe pumps, and a Matrix-esque black leather trench.

    Jenner also opted for a little black dress during Paris Fashion Week in January. She was photographed for an evening in the French city wearing a long-sleeve midi dress featuring a thigh slit and strapped buckles at the torso. She further elevated the look with sky-high leather boots and a slick side-parted hairdo.
