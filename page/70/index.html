Gear Up (move) - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

14 Sep 2024, 00:00

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Gear Up (Japanese: アシストギア Assist Gear) is a non-damaging Steel-type move introduced in Generation VII. Effect Gear Up raises the Attack and Special Attack stats of allied Pokémon (including the user) with the Ability Plus or Minus by one stage each. If powered up by a Steelium Z into Z-Gear Up, the user's Special Attack stat rises one stage. Generation VIII Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl Gear Up cannot be selected in a battle.
