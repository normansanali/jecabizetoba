    Actor Jason Statham is a martial artist who has become one of the most successful Hollywood stars on the planet. His popularity skyrocketed after joining the Fast and Furious franchise, and Statham even had a near-death experience while filming. However, his fame in action flicks led many fans to question his personal life. Although The Meg actor is notoriously private, his long-term partner Rosie Huntington-Whiteley has never been shy in talking about their personal life.

    Before the two met in 2009, Jason Statham made headlines for another famous fling. It turns out that Jason Statham's age-gap romance with Rosie isn't his first Hollywood relationship. Here's what we know about Jason Statham's current and past relationships, plus what Jason makes headlines for while keeping his relationships quiet.

    Jason Statham Is In A Long-Term Relationship With Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

    The couple met in 2009 and became engaged in 2016

    Jason Statham met Rosie Huntington-Whiteley in 2009 after meeting her at a party. In the early days of their relationship, the couple kept things very private.

    Despite the couple's 20-year age gap, they seem to be happy together. Plus, Rosie has her own massive net worth and doesn't need Jason's.

    In 2013, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley opened up on what she loved about Jason Statham.

    "His knowledge and strength are really inspiring and attractive, and that can come with a man who's had a bit of time," she told Glamour at the time.

    "We have a connection that has nothing to do with age. Work comes and goes, but if you have someone at home who thinks you're the greatest person in the world, that keeps you going."

    Is Jason Statham Actually Tough In Real Life?
    Fans love Jason Statham's tough-guy act on screen, but they wonder whether he's hard-as-nails in real life.

    Only one year after their engagement, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Jason Statham welcomed their first baby in June 2017. In an Instagram post at the time, the proud mom revealed that they named their son Jack Oscar Statham. She went on to tell Extra that Jason Statham was loving life as a dad.

    “He is very happy to get up at 5 in the morning and do daddy duty and let me sleep an extra hour," she said at the time. "He's very hands-on, he's a brilliant dad. I am very lucky, he's a great partner."

    In February 2022, Jason Statham and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley welcomed a daughter that they named Isabella James Statham.

    Jason Statham Had Been Involved In Several High-Profile Relationships

    Jason Statham was previously engaged to Kelly Brook, but their relationship only lasted from 1998 to 2004

    Jason Statham's ex-girlfriend, model Kelly Brook, did not have good things to say about the actor, who she dated from 1998 to 2004. Kelly Brook said her relationship with Jason Statham was “non-memorable” even though they were even engaged at one point.

    Kelly Brook accused Jason Statham of greed after they called it quits, saying, “He was skint when I was there, trust me.” Kelly Brook later went on to date actor Billy Zane and Nicole Scherzinger’s partner, Thom Evans.

    Jason Statham was also linked to Alex Zosman from 2006 to 2010. The Hollywood actress is best known for her roles in films like Shut up and Kiss Me. Even though the two Hollywood stars connected over their love of acting, they managed to keep their relationship out of the spotlight.

    Statham also had a brief relationship with actress, pop singer, and model Sophie Monk. The two dated briefly in 2005 but seemed to call it quits by the end of the year. Monk is best known for winning the reality show Popstars in 2000, which helped launch her career.

    Jason Statham Has Seen His Fair Share Of Drama

    Even though he is notoriously private, Jason Statham has been involved in several controversies

    The Crank actor has been through his fair share of controversies. Jason Statham was once kicked out of the infamous Playboy mansion by Hugh Hefner himself.

    Jason Statham is very careful about his public image and does not like to be photographed often. After Statham kept turning down photos with models, Hugh Hefner allegedly kicked Statham out of the infamous Playboy mansion.

    "The Bank Job actor was escorted off Hugh Hefner's plush Los Angeles premises after he refused to pose for pictures with models at the annual Midsummer Night's Dream Party,” the publication revealed.

    "The British star - who attended the bash wearing only a bathrobe - was escorted out by five security guards."

    Here's What Jason Statham Did Before He Was An Actor
    Statham was 31 before he got his first major role in a feature film - as Bacon in 1998's Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels.

    That wasn’t the only time that the actor was embroiled in controversy. Jennifer Lopez admitted that filming an intimate scene with Jason Statham was “traumatic.”

    Although JLo has been involved in many Hollywood productions, it appears that filming 2013’s Parker was one of the most challenging roles of her career.

    “My heart was beating out of my chest,” JLo recalled. “It was awful. You can hear a pin drop. And everybody knows [the scene is] coming. It was really nerve-wracking.” Although she was not forced to take all of her clothes off, she said that it was “enough to make me feel uncomfortable.”

    “You do – you get so nervous, and he’s sitting there. I’m like, ‘This is so weird,’ But… I knew it was part of the script, and we talked about how far we would go and, ‘Is he gonna make me take everything off?’” she continued.

    Parker received mixed reviews at the time of its release, and the film led to speculation that JLo and Jason Statham were an item at one point — but that was never confirmed by either party.
