    Cody Calafiore has carved a niche for himself as a distinguished American reality television personality, seamlessly transitioning between roles as an actor and producer for over a decade. His journey in the entertainment industry commenced with the debut role of Cody in the 2014 TV series “The Bold and the Beautiful.”

    However, it was his captivating stint as a houseguest in the sixteenth season of Big Brother that propelled him into the limelight, captivating audiences with his strategic gameplay. His prowess in the game reached its zenith when he clinched victory in Big Brother 22: All-Stars in 2020. The reality star further showcased his acting skills in notable films such as “Clinton Street” and “Set It Up.”

    Cody Calafiore’s Love Story: Engaged to Cristie LaRatta

    Cody Calafiore’s romantic journey has been intertwined with Cristie LaRatta for an extended period. The couple embarked on their journey of love in September 2015, and over the years, they have shared the highs and lows of life, captivating fans with their heartwarming connection.

    The pinnacle of their love story came to fruition when Cody Calafiore proposed to Cristie LaRatta in the picturesque locale of Red Bank, New Jersey, in October 2022. The proposal, adorned with a bouquet and a heartfelt note, marked a significant milestone in their relationship. As of now, the couple is engaged, and fans are eagerly awaiting official confirmation of their impending nuptials.

    While details about their wedding plans remain undisclosed, the engagement has sparked excitement, leaving room for speculation about a romantic ceremony that might unfold soon.

    Family and Future: Cody and Cristie’s Journey

    Cody Calafiore and Cristie LaRatta, as of now, have not welcomed any children into their lives. The couple, basking in the joy of their engagement, may contemplate expanding their family after the culmination of their wedding ceremony.

    In terms of family background, Cody is the son of Paul Calafiore, although information about his mother remains private. Growing up, he shared his childhood with two siblings, Paulie Calafiore and Angelina Calafiore.
