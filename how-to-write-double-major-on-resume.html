    How to write double major on resume

    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    How do you write double major on resume?

    Include the full name of your degree, major(s), minor(s), emphases, and certificates on your resume. Double Majors – You will not be receiving two bachelor’s degrees if you double major. Your primary major determines the degree (Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science).

    How do you write a double major BA?

    You should list your double major as a single entry, unless you attained two separate degrees (i.e. B.A. vs. B.S.). However, if you earned a double major beneath one degree, simply include the words “Double Major” before listing out both majors earned.

    How do you write double degree after your name?

    Start the entry with the name of the college or university you attended, followed by the degree you earned and the year you earned it. For a double major, a common styling is: Big State College, Bachelor of Arts – Double Major in Psychology and Education, 2008.

    How do you write your degree after your name?

    In general reference to a type of degree, lowercase the name/level of the degree, and in some cases, use the possessive (not plural) form. In a sentence that mentions a degree earned by an individual, spell out and lowercase the name of the degree on first reference; abbreviate it thereafter.
