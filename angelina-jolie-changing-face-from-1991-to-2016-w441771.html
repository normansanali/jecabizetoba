    Still stunning! Angelina Jolie has been in the movie business since 1982, when she had a small role in her father Jon Voight’s film Lookin’ to Get Out. And more than 30 years later, the Oscar-winning actress is still as stunning as ever — but she’s gone through quite the evolution. Watch Jolie’s face change through the years in the video above!

    Jolie, 41, who announced her split from husband Brad Pitt on September 20, started out as the quintessential ’80s child (puffed sleeves and all). Then she officially started getting into movies in the early 1990s, sporting her signature brunette tresses styled into feathered waves. 

    After that, the Mr. & Mrs. Smith actress experimented with lots of styles, including a pixie cut (buzzed for her role in 1998’s Gia), blonde curls and then goth makeup (circa 2000, when she picked up her Best Supporting Actress Oscar for Girl, Interrupted). And that brings Us to the ultra-glamorous Jolie of today — a global movie star, director and humanitarian who, on the red carpet, prefers a glossy blowout with an understated makeup palette.
