    What Is Tucky Belt And How Does It Work?

    The Tucky Belt is a new undergarment accessory that allows a person to tuck in any shirt without adding bulk. It’s constructed of thick, sumptuous Jacquard elastic coated with silicone strips for added traction.

    The Tucky Belt is entirely adjustable from size 00 to size 14, making it suitable for wear by everyone. The Tucky Belt is simple: unhook the clasp, adjust the slider to completely extend the Tucky, close the clip, and fold your shirt up and under the band for a long-lasting tuck.

    Brooke Knaus designed the Tucky Belt while experimenting with her wardrobe during the 2020 pandemic and sought a comfortable solution to get a sophisticated look.
