    Triple H missed Raw on Monday because his brother in law Gordon who was married to his sister Lynn passed away. Here is his obituary from the local newspaper:

    Published: Wednesday, February 6, 2008
    Gordon B. Ouellette
    Gordon B. Ouellette, 41, of Monica Dr., Nashua passed away unexpectedly Monday February 4, 2008 at Southern NH Medical Center.

    Mr. Ouellette was born in Tewksbury, MA on April 17, 1965 a son of Ralph Ouellette of Gilford and Mary (Meisner) Ouellette of Nashua. He was the husband of Lynn (Levesque) Ouellette of Nashua.

    Gordon had been a longtime resident of Nashua. Mr. Ouellette had been employed by Maynard & Paquette in Nashua. He enjoyed motorcycling, ice fishing and hunting.

    Besides his wife, Gordon is survived by one son, Peter Ouellette of Nashua; one daughter, Neysa Ouellette of Nashua; his father, Ralph Ouellette and his wife Susan of Gilford; his mother, Mary Ouellette and her partner Ellen Svenson of Nashua; also several cousins.

    SERVICES: Visiting hours will be held on Friday February 8, 2008 from 4 to 8 PM in the Anctil-Rochette & Son Funeral Home, 21 Kinsley St., Nashua.

    A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Saturday February 9, 2008 in St. Louis de Gonzague Church, 48 West Hollis St., Nashua at 9:30 AM. Burial will follow in St. Louis Cemetery.
