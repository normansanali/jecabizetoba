    More Success and Film Earnings

    After the conclusion of the Harry Potter saga, Emma Watson continued to excel in the entertainment industry. She starred in several successful films, showcasing her versatility as an actress. One of her notable projects was “Beauty and the Beast,” where she portrayed the iconic character of Belle. This film was a box office hit, earning over $1.2 billion worldwide. Watson’s involvement in the film contributed significantly to her net worth.

In total, Watson’s earnings from “Beauty and the Beast” amounted to $15 million. This not only highlights her talent as an actress but also her ability to command high salaries for her roles.
