    Kid Cudi addressed his recently released feature on a Pusha T track.

    “Hey! So I know some of you heard about the song I got w Pusha. I did this song a year ago when I was still cool w Kanye. I am not cool w that man. He’s not my friend and I only cleared the song for Pusha cuz thats my guy. This is the last song u will hear me on w Kanye -Scott” he tweeted.

    In February Kanye announced that Kid Cudi would not appear on his upcoming 11th studio album Donda 2, sharing the news in a handwritten note posted to Instagram on Saturday.

    “Just so everyone knows Cudi will not be on Donda because he’s friends with you-know-who,” West wrote. “We all speak in Billie language now.”

    Kanye was upset that Cudi is friends with Pete Davidson.

    In a comment captured by the @commentsbycelebs Instagram account, Cudi, 38, responded: “Too bad I don’t wanna be on ur album u f—– dinosaur hahaha. Everyone knows I’ve been the best thing about ur albums since I met u. Ima pray u for [sic] brother.”
