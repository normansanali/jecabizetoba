Despite being born in Guyana, Wright’s family relocated to London when she was seven years old and raised her there. She is 30 years old and was born under the sign of Scorpio.

    Letitia Wright made her television debut in 2011 with the role of Ellie Maynard in ‘Holby City.’ During the same year, she also appeared in the TV movie ‘Random’ and four episodes of the Top Boy crime series. Furthermore, she starred as Nish in the science fiction anthology TV series ‘Black Mirror’ in 2017, and she is currently working on her upcoming series ‘Small Axe.’
