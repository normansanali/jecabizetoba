    Who is Molly Ephraim? | Molly Ephraim Biography and Wiki

    Molly Ephraim born May 22, 1986, is an American actress who has shown up in films, television, and Broadway, Off-Broadway, and regional theater productions.     Molly Ephraim Last Man Standing

    Ephraim showed up as a series regular in the role of Mandy Baxter, daughter of series’ lead Mike Baxter (portrayed by Tim Allen), on the ABC sitcom Last Man Standing, which premiered on October 11, 2011, and originally ended in March 2017, canceled after six seasons. The series was revived by Fox in May 2018, but Ephraim opted not to return for the show’s seventh season, with the role being recast.
