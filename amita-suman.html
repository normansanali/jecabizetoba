    Amita Suman Age

    Amita Suman age is 30 years old, as of 2023. She was born on January 27, 1993. She was born in southern region of Nepal, to a Bhojpuri-speaking family, and is also fluent in the language herself. As per her educational qualifications, she studied Performing Arts at the Lewes campus of East Sussex College, from 2013-2015. She studied at The Academy of Live & Recorded Arts, after college. Suman moved to Brighton, England, at the age of seven.

    The first thing Suman did when she arrived in Brighton was to learn English. She stated, ‘I found it hard to make friends because I was so introverted and so insecure and I was so aware of how different I was that I couldn’t talk to people, so I didn’t know what British culture was like.
