    Beyoncé brought her disco fantasia Renaissance to life in Paris.

    The superstar has shared an inside look at the exclusive Fashion Week afterparty where she celebrated the release of her latest album last Tuesday. For the event, the "Cuff It" singer chose two different looks: a sophisticated all-maroon set by Saint Laurent and a sparkling silver metallic look.

    The shimmering outfit was comprised of a plunging halter top with chain detailing and matching metallic fabric hood, a pair of silver flare-leg trousers, and an oversized foiled biker jacket. Her accessories included an array of Tiffany & Co. jewelry, featuring a statement scorpion necklace, and a pair of oversized square sunglasses.

    In addition to the stunning outfits, the singer's Instagram post also includes a peek at "Club Renaissance," in a silent clip where the DJ is set in front of a massive cut-out of the star wearing one of her album artwork looks.

    For her second look of the night, the pop star wore a sleek long leather coat paired with skintight halter dress with a draped hood and triangle cutout in the bust, both from Saint Laurent's spring 2023 collection. She finished look with slingback pumps from the French luxury house, also in maroon, as well as black rectangle sunglasses and a white and maroon scarf.

    Beyoncé.com

    Beyoncé partnered with Tiffany & Co. on both the Paris bash and a recent promotional music video, titled "Lose Yourself in Love" and set to "Summer Renaissance." The '70s inspired clip was released on October 3, nearly two months after the star dropped the first teaser for Renaissance's visuals in August.

    The two-minute "I'M THAT GIRL." video showed the singer wearing several futuristic looks, before a rapid montage showcased an array of different Beyoncés flashing onscreen. In true Parkwood fashion, there has been no confirmation on when further visuals will be released.
