    Yuzuru Hanyu is a Japanese figure skater. As of 2023, Yuzuru Hanyu’s net worth is $2 million. He is the first Asian man to win an Olympic gold medal in men’s singles skating. He was the youngest male skater to win the Olympic championship since Dick Button in 1948 at the age of nineteen.

    He also became the first guy since Button’s back-to-back Olympic gold medals in 1948 and 1952 to win two straight gold medals. Hanyu became the first skater in history to successfully perform a quadruple loop during a competition at the 2016 CS Autumn Classic International.
