    Career and Professional Life

    2013 – 2016

It was a nomination at the 2014 MBC Drama Awards in the category “Best New Actress”.

    Yea Ji followed this with an appearance in Super Daddy Yeol in 2015. Additionally, she garnered the attention of the viewer’s attention through her role in Last. Following this, the actress made her film debut with The Throne in 2015. In this historical drama film, Yea Ji portrayed the role of Queen Jeongsun.

    Subsequently, the actress appeared in the film Circle of Atonement the same year. The same year, Yea Ji also appeared in the music video of Let’s Not Fall In Love. It is a song by the famous South Korean boy band Big Bang.

    In the year 2016, Yea Ji appeared as one of the main characters in Moorim School: Saga of the Brave. She then starred as a supporting character in Seondal: The Man Who Sells the River. The same year, the actress began working on the new project Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth which aired from December 19, 2016, to February 21, 2017.

In the year 2021, Yea Ji worked on a new project, i.e. the film Recalled.
