    Jaire Alexander Height: How Tall is Jaire Alexander? – Jaire Alexander is a prominent American football cornerback currently playing for the Green Bay Packers in the National Football League (NFL).

    Born on February 9, 1997, and early in his life, Alexander moved from Philadelphia to the Charlotte area as a toddler. He attended Rocky River High School in Mint Hill, North Carolina, playing football for the Ravens. Initially committing to the University of South Carolina, he later changed his commitment to the University of Louisville.
