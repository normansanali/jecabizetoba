    Beloved comedian and actor Tone Bell has garnered many fans over the years thanks to his contributions to the entertainment industry. Hence, his role as the host of Netflix’s ‘Drink Masters‘ made many in the public quite happy. Those who were not familiar with the artist’s work prior to the show got to see just how talented and funny the entertainer is. His punches and comments during the competitive events in the reality show helped break the ice and put the contestants at ease. The actor also provided the viewpoint of a casual drink enjoyer in a room full of alcohol experts with much grace. All of this has made a lot of people curious about the comedian’s professional achievements and his present net worth, and we are here to tell you what we know about it.

    How Did Tone Bell Earn His Money?

    Michael Anthony Bell II AKA Tone Bell is a popular stand-up comedian and actor. The Georgia native used to teach first grade before he took up a production role in Joseph Sargent’s ‘Warm Springs,’ along with working on promo materials. He then moved to San Francisco, California, and started working as a brand manager for Swivel Media. However, Bell moved back to Atlanta after a year and resumed promotion work, this time for a beer company, which led him to shift to Dallas, Texas. While in Dallas, Bell started frequenting comedy clubs and utilized open mic events to showcase his comedic skills.

    In order to further himself in the entertainment industry, Tone Bell moved to Los Angeles, California, where he claimed the title of winner in the 2012 NBC Stand Up For Diversity Talent Search. This secured him a developmental deal with NBC and paved his path for essaying roles like RJ in ‘Whitney’ and Tedward Mulray in ‘Bad Judge.’ Some of his other major television roles include ‘Single Ladies,’ ‘Truth Be Told,’ ‘Disjointed,’ ‘Fam,’ and ‘American Soul.’ He has also been a part of ‘Key & Peele,’ ‘The Flash,’ ‘BoJack Horseman,’ and ‘Nailed It!‘ In October 2022, Bell graced Netflix’s ‘Drink Masters’ as a host.

    Thomas Verrette’s 2013 film, ‘Implanted,’ features Bell as Philip and helped him make an impact within the film industry. Bell has also showcased his skills in ‘Dog Days,’ ‘The United States vs. Billie Holiday,’ ‘Sylvie’s Love,’ and ‘Little.’ Bell can also be seen in ‘The Green Room,’ an internet series by the YouTube channel All Def. The series also stars Sydney Castillo and James Davis. In 2019, Tone Bell starred in ‘Tone Bell: Can’t Cancel This,’ a comedy special for which he also acted as a producer. He also has a comedy album titled ‘One Night in Austin.’ The comedian also has his own line of clothing in partnership with Wrighteous, a clothing brand.
