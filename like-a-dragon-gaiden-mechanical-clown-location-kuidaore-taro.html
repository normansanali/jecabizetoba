    If you’re looking for the mechanical clown location in Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name, here’s how to complete the Osaka’s Famous Clown quest.

    This clown is located in Kuidaore Taro, in the middle of Sotenbori Street. Check the stores on the upper side of the street and you’ll spot it playing a drum outside of a store.

    Let’s explain this in further detail, including its exact map location and what kind of rewards to expect from this quest.

    Mechanical Clown Location in Like a Dragon Gaiden

    To find the Mechanical Clown in Like a Dragon Gaiden, head to Kuidaore Taro, in the middle of Sotenbori Street. Check the stores on the north side and you’ll be able to spot it just outside a store.

    If you are struggling to find it, check the image above for reference.

    It will be a mechanic clown playing a drum in a white and red costume. Take a picture of it and you’ll complete the quest if Kiryu says the following:

    “I took the photograph I was asked for… I should report back.”

    This will allow you to complete the Osaka’s Famous Clown quest, which will reward you with 500 Akame network Points and 10,000 yen. The reward is definitely worth it, considering this only takes a minute to complete.

    Wrapping up

    To summarize, you can find the Mechanical Clown in LADG: TMWEHN by checking the front of the stores on the upper side of Sotenbori Street.

    Take a picture of it and that’s all you need to complete this quest and make some easy money.
