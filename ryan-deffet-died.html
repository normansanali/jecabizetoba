    Who was Ryan Deffet?

    Ryan Deffet was a resident of Chicago, Illinois, but originally hailed from Dayton, Ohio. He held the role of Junior Media & Trade Ambassador at FAUX FURRS and served as a Gate Keeper at Space Gators. What made Ryan truly exceptional was his deep involvement in music, as he was part of at least three different bands. His presence was marked by unwavering sincerity, boundless passion, and an innate ability to inspire and uplift those around him.
