    The date 2/2/2022 “Twosday” was celebrated by many because it won’t happen again for another 400 years. But the Design Star: Next Gen judge Lauren Makk and Al Lozano had a different reason to celebrate — they got married in an intimate ceremony at the Alabaster Collective in Nashville that day!

    Let’s get to know Lauren’s husband better as this Al Lozano Bio proceeds. 

    Meet Al Lozano, Lauren Makk Husband

    Al Lozano and Lauren Makk officially started dating on December 2, 2019, after first meeting at a restaurant in Los Angeles. Recalling their first encounter, Lauren shared, “from the minute we saw each other, we knew there was something there.”

    It seems that the duo was the only customer in that restaurant that day. So, they stuck up a conversation, learned that they were both headed to Nashville in the coming days, and Al asked her out.

    Over 3 later that day, the duo then went on to tie the knot on Feb. 2, 2022, exactly after a year of engagement.

    Talking about their wedding, Lauren labeled it as “a night of indulgence.” The couple had a 360 photo booth, a DJ, and had their first dance as husband/wife to Maze’s Before I Let Go. “It was perfect,” she added.

    And guess who officiated the ceremony? Their couple therapists! Yes, in an intimate ceremony surrounded by 36 family and friends, Al and Makk were announced husband and wife by their therapists, whom they met early in their relationship.

    According to Lauren, they each bring “a lot of baggage to the table.” So, from that start, they wanted to embark on a healthy partnership together. Thus, the therapist.

    But the therapist officiating their wedding wasn’t the only thing that eschewed a traditional wedding party. The newlyweds also had their children participate in the ceremony. They were the ringbearers.

    After the wedding, Lauren then took it to her IG with a few wedding pics to share the good news captioned, “Allow me to reintroduce myself… Mrs. Lauren Lozano.”

    Also, she uploaded an IG clip from the 360 photo booth they had at the wedding, where they danced with their two little kids.

    Al Lozano Kids And Ex-Wife

    Al Lozano has four biological kids in total — three (two sons and daughter) from his previous marriage, and one with Lauren Makk named Lennox Avelino (born on March 25, 2020.)

    His son, Tre Wallace celebrated his 26th birthday on March 13, 2021, while his daughter turned 21 on May 28, 2021. His other kid graduated college in May 2015.

    Likewise, Lauren also has one son from her previous relationship named Preston Cruz (born in October 2016)

    As for Al’s ex-wife, she was reported to be Tsan Lozano, the owner of Flawless Concepts. She is an author, hairstylist, and a self-described “gluten-free foodie.” The last we checked, she resided in her hometown Nashville, Tennessee

    Al Lozano Age

    Al Lozano was 45 years of age when he got married to the Design Star: Next Gen judge in 2022.

    His wife, Lauren was 36 years then.

    Al Lozano Job

    Al Lozano is a motor vehicle technician who owned Nation Dent Repair. Reportedly, he opened the company in Mach 2009, after working over a decade at the Dent Wizard.

    According to Nation Dent Repair’s official FB page, they are a mobile service specializing in the repair of dents, dings, creases, and hail damage in vehicles. They’ve been providing paintless dent repair services in the greater Nashville area since 1999, and have many of Nashville’s dealerships, from Hendersonville to Franklin. 

    Besides this, Al also owned a recording label called GoldHouse. Back in 2014, their label dropped the lead single for artist “Villz.” He was an artist at the time. But thanks to their team, his single went on to have 100,000+ views on YouTube, 400,000+ plays on Sound Cloud, and constant rotation on MTVJams for over a year and a half.
