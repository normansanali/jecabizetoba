    Olivia Culpo is an American fashion influencer and a social media personality. She was born on 8 May 1992 in Cranston, Rhode Island, U.S. She is the winner of Miss Rhode Island U.S.A, Miss U.S.A and Miss Universe 2012. Her father name is Peter Culpo and mother name is Susan Culpo. She has 4 siblings.

    She did her education from St. Mary Academy – Bay View and Boston University. Other than modeling she also appeared in movies and tv serials. She made her debut in movies with The Other Woman in 2014 and in serials with Hell’s Kitchen in 2017.
