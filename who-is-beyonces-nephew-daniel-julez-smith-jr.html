    Being a teenager can be a trying period of our lives, with an endless amount of questions on how to navigate these tricky-but-tremendous years. Luckily for sixteen-year-old Daniel Julez Smith Jr., he has two notable family members who know a thing or two about navigating the teen years. In fact, both of these ladies have released a number of songs between them discussing a number of experiences, full of perspective!

    The two dynamic divas in Julez Smith Jr.'s life are his mother and aunt, who are both most commonly known professionally by one name; Julez's mother is Solange, and his aunt is no other than Miss. Beyonce Knowles! With having such a dynamic duo for a mother and aunt, it may feel easy for your identity to feel like it doesn't matter or your name is easily lost beneath the legacy of your famous family; This idea certainly does not apply to Julez. It's time for the world to get to know Julez Smith Jr.'s unique artistry!

    Getting To Know Julez

    Judging from his family name, it would be quite a fair assumption Julez is quite the accomplished teen with a supportive crew behind him; the Knowles family bond runs deep, especially between Julez and his mom! Solange gave birth to Julez when she was only seventeen years-old. Giving birth to Julez brought Solange a lot of comfort, especially in the circumstances she was facing at the time. While Solange was pregnant, she lost a friend to gun violence, and she had to reckon with the intricate concepts of life and death, as she explained in an intimate letter to her teenage self for Teen Vogue, via People.

    Solange's mama bear instincts were quickly instilled from the moment she brought Julez into the world with her former partner, Daniel Smith. In the aforementioned letter, she explained, "You will be terrified, and it's ok that you don't know what the future holds. Some people will count you out because of the decision you've made to bring another life into the world so young, but you made the decision out of love, and will live with the decision in love."

    Love continues to be the basis for which Solange leads her life with her son Julez. Solange is more on the private side when it comes to sharing intimate details of her family life on social media than her son who actively posts on Instagram, but she broke her silence when he received criticism for learning how to speak French.

    A young Julez studied French in school, and gave a speech when he graduated from the fifth grade, but, according to Essence, his ability to speak multiple languages didn't sit well for some fans. A fan commented their displeasure over the idea of Julez studying French instead of African American history, but Mama bear wasn't too pleased with the fan's unsolicited feedback. Solange defended her decision to have Julez study French and reiterated how "Proud [she] was of her soon-to-be sixth-grader."

    An Inside Look At Julez's Life Today

    Julez is quite the teen dream!

Julez's aforementioned Instagram account is a good source for gaining insight into who he really is, and how he enjoys spending his time.

    From doing a quick scroll on the 'Gram, fans can discover pictorial proof of Julez's familial bond. He has posted several photos of his mother Solange, and his aunt Beyonce; his uncle Jay-Z even makes an appearance!

    Like many guys his age, Julez enjoys the ladies! His private life has been a topic of interest through the years for his admires, and there have been a few select women who have on his arm. As of the Fall of last year, the woman who captured his heart also comes from a famous family. Remember former Kardashian-Jenner BFF Jordyn Woods? Her little sister caught Julez's eye, and according to News.amomama, he's quite fond of Jodie Woods, and even made things 'Instagram official' by posting pictures where he professes his love for her.

    Of course, it takes a few tries before one finds their one-and-only! Woods isn't the first woman to have captured Julez's heart.
