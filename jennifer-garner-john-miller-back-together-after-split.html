    JENNIFER Garner and John Miller have rekindled their romance after a nine-month split.

    The couple were first linked in April of 2018, and spent two years together before calling it quits.

    Jennifer, 49, and John, 43, are officially back on, a source told Us Weekly on Tuesday.

    A few weeks ago the pair decided to give it another go, despite breaking it off in August of 2020.

    “Jen and John are back on. It started up a few weeks ago," an insider revealed.

    In October of 2018, the news broke that Jen had been dating the CaliGroup CEO for about six months.

    Their romance formed just a few years after the actress finalized her divorce from ex-husband Ben Affleck in 2015.

    The famed couple spent 10 years married and share three children, daughters Violet, 15, and Serafina, 12, and son Samuel, 9.

    John was also previously married to classic musician and violinist Caroline Campbell, with whom he shares two kids.

    In August of 2019, a source disclosed that the 13 Going On 30 star connected with the businessman over parenting.

    “John and Jen are in similar places in their lives and can relate to raising kids after a divorce,” they explained.

    “They’re on the same page and understand where things stand.”

    However, just a year later they split when Jen was unable to commit to tying the knot.

    “He was ready for marriage and she just couldn’t commit," a source said at the time.

    “They parted on very amicable terms.”

    Despite the Alias star feeling “extremely content and blissfully happy” during their relationship, she was not ready to "rush" into anything.

    Jen and Ben Affleck finalized their divorce in October of 2018, though the Justice League actor was devastated by the decision.

    The actor spoke to The New York Times about their split in February of 2020, where he said: "The biggest regret of my life is this divorce.”

    “Shame is really toxic. There is no positive by-product of shame. It’s just stewing in a toxic, hideous feeling of low self-worth and self-loathing.”

    Since their divorce, Ben has dated SNL producer Lindsay Shookus, Playboy model Shauna Sexton, and Ana de Armas.

    Last week the Good Will Hunting star was spotted spending time with Jennifer Lopez, just weeks after she called off her engagement to Alex Rodriguez.

    On Friday, The Sun reported how Ben, 48, and JLo were "secretly hanging out at her LA home."

    An insider told People that this is a normal occurrence and not out of the ordinary, despite the pair calling off their engagement in the early 2000's.

    "They are friends. They have always been friends and they have seen each other through the years."

    Ben proposed to JLo in 2002, but allegedly the paparazzi and tabloids surrounding the engagement were so extreme, that they called it off in 2004.

    An insider told Page Six that the exes have been spending extra time together since her split from ARod, and he's been seen going to her L.A. home several times.
