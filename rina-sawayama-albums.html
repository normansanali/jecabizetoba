    Rina Sawayama Albums: Rina Sawayama is a Japanese-British singer, actress, and model born on August 16, 1990.

    She developed a passion for singing at a younger age and at the age of sixteen, Sawayama began posting music covers on Myspace.

    During sixth form, she formed a hip-hop group called Lazy Lion and later began her solo career in February 2013, with the release of the single; “Sleeping in Waking”.

    Sawayama has since then been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after British singers.

    Known for her musical versatility, she has released several singles as well as videos and is a recipient of several nominations, awards, and accolades.

    As a model, she’s signed to Anti-Agency and Elite Model Management, and in 2017, she was chosen for Versus x Versace’s fall/winter campaign.

    Sawayama has modeled for fashion campaigns and has also starred in campaigns for Jourdan Dunn’s Missguided

    She walked the runway for Balmain to celebrate the ten-year anniversary of Olivier Rousteing being the head of the French House.

    Sawayama was featured on the cover of British Vogue in June 2023 and also made her film acting debut in the action film John Wick: Chapter 4 (2023)

    Since making her debut, Rina Sawayama has served the music community with two studio albums.

    On April 17, 2020, she released her debut studio album, “Sawayama” and on September 16, 2022, her second studio album, “Hold The Girl” was released.
